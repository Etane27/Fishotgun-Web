About The Game

Tiny pond vibes, very questionable fishing methods.

Fishotgun is a multiplayer or solo fishing game set in a bright, innocent-looking world inspired by cozy life sims and playful low-poly landscapes.

Instead of using a rod, players blast fish out of the water, collect what they catch, and slowly build up a growing collection filled with different classes and rarities.

It is also built as a social experience, with public lobbies and chat systems that let players hang out, explore, and create chaos together between fishing sessions.
